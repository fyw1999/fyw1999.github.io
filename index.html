I am currently a Ph.D. student at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Sun Yat-sen University (SYSU), advised by Prof. Andy J. Ma.

My research interests include computer vision and AI agents, especially reinforcement-learning post-training for multi-turn agents.

🔥 News

  • [May 2026]  We released “Train the Agent, Not the Expert: Learning to Harness Heterogeneous Experts for Multi-Turn Visual Reasoning.”
  • [Jan. 2026]  We released the MovingDroneCrowd++ benchmark and methods for video individual counting and tracking from moving drones.
  • [Oct. 2025]  Our paper “Video Individual Counting for Moving Drones” was presented at ICCV 2025 and selected as an ICCV 2025 Highlight.
  • [Jan. 2025]  Our paper “Learning Crowd Scale and Distribution for Weakly Supervised Crowd Counting and Localization” was published in IEEE TCSVT.
